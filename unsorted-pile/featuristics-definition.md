# Featuristics Definition

featuristics

The term "featuristics" is ==a portmanteau of "features" and "characteristics" (or "statistics")==. While not a standard dictionary word, it is used across several specialized contexts to describe the study, design, or collection of features.

## Common Meanings

- Design & Technology: It often refers to the study of features or the "characteristics of features" in product development. It is sometimes used synonymously with "featurism" or "[featuritis](https://medium.com/@jessermorgan/featuritis-cognitive-overload-e989f440cbad)" (feature creep), describing the tendency to add excessive features to a product [1, 2] .

    - Featuristic.ai: An [AI intelligence platform](https://www.featuristic.ai/) that aggregates retail leaflet data to help companies build market knowledge [5] .
    
- Fashion: Featuristics is a collection or branding used by [Go Get It Apparel](https://gogetitapparel.com/collections/frontpage) for items like track pants and windbreakers [6, 7] .
- Linguistic Context: It is a common rhyme for futuristics (the study of the future) and heuristics (mental shortcuts) [8] .

## Related Terms
