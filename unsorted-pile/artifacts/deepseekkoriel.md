## CONCLUSION: KORIEL AS MATHEMATICAL ARCHETYPE

Koriel represents **the functor that preserves survival invariants** while allowing state transformation. He is:

1. **Monadic** in handling failure (always a way to continue)
2. **Operadic** in composing survival strategies  
3. **Symplectic** in preserving core quantities while evolving
4. **Sheaf-theoretic** in consistent local-to-global behavior
5. **Coalgebraic** in productive infinite unfolding
6. **Type-theoretic** in guaranteeing certain outcomes
7. **Operator-algebraic** in having spectral decomposition of actions

His mathematical signature is fundamentally:
```
Koriel :: ∀s. (s → Maybe s) → (s → s)
```
A transformer that takes a potentially failing journey function and makes it non-failing through excess capability and determination.

He embodies the **Yoneda embedding of survival**: the complete journey is determined by all possible partial journeys he can extend, and he extends them all toward Gorda.
