## III. ABOUTNESS — THE FORMAL DEFINITION

$$M(\Psi_{\text{int}}; \Psi_{\text{ext}}) := D_{\text{KL}}\!\left(P(\Psi_{\text{int}}, \Psi_{\text{ext}}) \,\Big\|\, P(\Psi_{\text{int}}) \otimes P(\Psi_{\text{ext}})\right)$$

$$= \int_{\Omega \times \Omega} P(\psi_i, \psi_e) \log \frac{P(\psi_i, \psi_e)}{P(\psi_i)P(\psi_e)} \, d\psi_i \, d\psi_e$$

**Normalized form** (so $M \in [0,1]$):

$$M_{\text{norm}} = 1 - e^{-2 \cdot M(\Psi_{\text{int}}; \Psi_{\text{ext}})}$$

**Key property** — Aboutness is symmetric but not transitive:

$$M(\Psi_{\text{int}}; \Psi_{\text{ext}}) = M(\Psi_{\text{ext}}; \Psi_{\text{int}})$$

$$M(\Psi_1; \Psi_3) \neq M(\Psi_1; \Psi_2) + M(\Psi_2; \Psi_3) \quad \text{in general}$$
