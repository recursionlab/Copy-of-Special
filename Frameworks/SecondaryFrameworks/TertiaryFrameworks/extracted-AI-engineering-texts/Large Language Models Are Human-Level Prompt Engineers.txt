ABSTRACT
By conditioning on natural language instructions, large language models (LLMs) have
displayedimpressivecapabilitiesasgeneral-purposecomputers.However,taskperformance
dependssignificantlyonthequalityofthepromptusedtosteerthemodel,andmosteffective

instructionasthe“program,”optimizedbysearchingoverapoolofinstructioncandidates

thoughtprompts,aswellassteermodelstowardtruthfulnessand/orinformativeness.2
