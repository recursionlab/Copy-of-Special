### Etymology 1

From [Middle English](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_English "w:Middle English") *[langage](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/langage#Middle_English "langage")*, *[language](#Middle_English)*, from [Old French](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_French "w:Old French") *[language](#Old_French)*, from [Vulgar Latin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vulgar_Latin "w:Vulgar Latin") *[\*linguāticum](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Latin/linguaticum "Reconstruction:Latin/linguaticum")*, from [Latin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latin "w:Latin") *[lingua](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/lingua#Latin "lingua")* (“tongue, speech, language”), from [Old Latin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_Latin "w:Old Latin") *[dingua](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/dingua#Latin "dingua")* (“tongue”), from [Proto-Indo-European](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proto-Indo-European_language "w:Proto-Indo-European language") *[\*dn̥ǵʰwéh₂s](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/dn%CC%A5%C7%B5%CA%B0w%C3%A9h%E2%82%82s "Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/dn̥ǵʰwéh₂s")* (“tongue, speech, language”). [Doublet](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#doublet "Appendix:Glossary") of *[langaj](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/langaj#English "langaj")*. Displaced native [Old English](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_English "w:Old English") *[ġeþēode](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/ge%C3%BEeode#Old_English "geþeode")*.

#### Noun

**language** (*[countable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#countable "Appendix:Glossary") and [uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")*, *plural* **[languages](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/languages#English "languages")**)

1. ([countable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#countable "Appendix:Glossary")) A [body](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/body#English "body") of [words](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/word#English "word"), and set of methods of combining them (called a [grammar](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/grammar#English "grammar")), understood by a [community](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/community#English "community") and used as a form of [communication](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/communication#English "communication"). quotations ▼
	*The English and German **languages** are both members of the West Germanic **language** family.*
	*Deaf and mute people communicate using sign **language**.*
2. ([uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")) The ability to communicate using words. quotations ▼
	*the gift of **language***
3. ([uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")) A [sublanguage](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sublanguage#English "sublanguage"): the [slang](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/slang#English "slang") of a [particular](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/particular#English "particular") [community](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/community#English "community") or [jargon](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/jargon#English "jargon") of a [particular](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/particular#English "particular") [specialist](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/specialist#English "specialist") [field](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/field#English "field"). quotations ▼
	*legal **language**; the **language** of chemistry*
4. ([uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")) The specific wording or style of a text, such as a law or a contract. quotations ▼
5. ([countable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#countable "Appendix:Glossary"), [uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary"), [figurative](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#figurative "Appendix:Glossary")) The expression of thought (the communication of meaning) in a specified way; that which communicates something, as language does. quotations ▼
	*body **language**; the **language** of the eyes*
6. ([countable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#countable "Appendix:Glossary"), [uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")) A [body](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/body#English "body") of [sounds](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sound#English "sound"), [signs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sign#English "sign") or [signals](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/signal#English "signal") by which [animals](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/animal#English "animal") [communicate](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/communicate#English "communicate"), and by which [plants](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/plant#English "plant") are sometimes also [thought](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/thought#English "thought") to [communicate](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/communicate#English "communicate"). quotations ▼
7. ([computing](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/computing#Noun "computing"), [countable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#countable "Appendix:Glossary")) A [computer language](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/computer_language#English "computer language"); a [machine language](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/machine_language#English "machine language"). quotations ▼
8. ([uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")) A [manner](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/manner#English "manner") of [expression](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/expression#English "expression"). quotations ▼
9. ([uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")) The particular words used in a [speech](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/speech#English "speech") or a [passage](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/passage#English "passage") of [text](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/text#English "text").
	*The **language** used in the law does not permit any other interpretation.*
	*The **language** he used to talk to me was obscene.*
10. ([euphemistic](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#euphemism "Appendix:Glossary"), [uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")) [Profanity](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/profanity#English "profanity"). quotations ▼
