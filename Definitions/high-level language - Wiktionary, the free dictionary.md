### Noun

\[[edit](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=high-level_language&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Noun")\]

1. ([programming](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/programming#Noun "programming")) A [programming language](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/programming_language#English "programming language") requiring a [compiler](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/compiler#English "compiler") to translate it into a form that a particular machine understands, focusing on [user-friendly](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/user-friendly#English "user-friendly") code development by automating core tasks such as accessing [memory](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/memory#English "memory"). alternative form, coordinate terms ▲
	Alternative form: (initialism) [HLL](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/HLL#English "HLL")
	Coordinate terms: [intermediate language](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/intermediate_language#English "intermediate language"), [low-level language](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/low-level_language#English "low-level language"), [assembly language](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/assembly_language#English "assembly language"), [machine language](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/machine_language#English "machine language")
