### Etymology 1

\[[edit](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=thinking&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Etymology 1")\]

From [Middle English](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_English "w:Middle English") *[thinking](#Middle_English)*, *[thynkynge](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=thynkynge&action=edit&redlink=1 "thynkynge (page does not exist)")*, *[thenkyng](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=thenkyng&action=edit&redlink=1 "thenkyng (page does not exist)")*, equivalent to + *[\-ing](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/-ing#English "-ing")*.

#### Noun

\[[edit](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=thinking&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Noun")\]

**thinking** (*usually [uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")*, *plural* **[thinkings](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/thinkings#English "thinkings")**)

1. The [action](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/action#English "action") or [process](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/process#English "process") of using one's [mind](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/mind#English "mind") to [consider](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/consider#English "consider") or [reason](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/reason#English "reason") about something.
2. A [thought](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/thought#English "thought"). quotations ▼
	*What is your **thinking** on this subject?*

### Etymology 2

\[[edit](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=thinking&action=edit&section=8 "Edit section: Etymology 2")\]

From [Middle English](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_English "w:Middle English") *[thenkinge](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=thenkinge&action=edit&redlink=1 "thenkinge (page does not exist)")*, *[þinkynge](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=%C3%BEinkynge&action=edit&redlink=1 "þinkynge (page does not exist)")*, *[þenkynge](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=%C3%BEenkynge&action=edit&redlink=1 "þenkynge (page does not exist)")*, *[þenchinde](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=%C3%BEenchinde&action=edit&redlink=1 "þenchinde (page does not exist)")*, from [Old English](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_English "w:Old English") *[þenċende](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%C3%BEencende#Old_English "þencende")*, from [Proto-Germanic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proto-Germanic_language "w:Proto-Germanic language") *[\*þankijandz](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEankijandz&action=edit&redlink=1 "Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/þankijandz (page does not exist)")*, present participle of *[\*þankijaną](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEankijan%C4%85 "Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/þankijaną")* (“to think”), equivalent to + *[\-ing](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/-ing#English "-ing")*. Cognate with [Dutch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dutch_language "w:Dutch language") *[denkend](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/denkend#Dutch "denkend")* (“thinking”), [German](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/German_language "w:German language") *[denkend](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/denkend#German "denkend")* (“thinking”), [Swedish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swedish_language "w:Swedish language") *[tänkande](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/t%C3%A4nkande#Swedish "tänkande")* (“thinking”).

**thinking**
