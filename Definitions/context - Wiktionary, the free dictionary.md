### Noun

**context** (*[countable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#countable "Appendix:Glossary") and [uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")*, *plural* **[contexts](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/contexts#English "contexts")**)

1. The [surroundings](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/surrounding#English "surrounding"), [circumstances](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/circumstance#English "circumstance"), [environment](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/environment#English "environment"), [background](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/background#English "background") or [settings](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/setting#English "setting") that determine, specify, or clarify the meaning of an [event](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/event#English "event") or other occurrence. synonyms ▲ quotations ▼
	Synonyms: [frame](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/frame#English "frame"), [framework](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/framework#English "framework"), [background](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/background#English "background"), [backdrop](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/backdrop#English "backdrop"), [setting](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/setting#English "setting"), [purlieus](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/purlieus#English "purlieus"), [milieu](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/milieu#English "milieu")
	*—In what **context** did your attack on him happen? —We had a pretty tense relationship at the time, and when he insulted me I snapped.*
2. ([linguistics](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/linguistics#English "linguistics")) The [text](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/text#English "text") in which a [word](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/word#English "word") or [passage](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/passage#English "passage") appears and which helps [ascertain](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/ascertain#English "ascertain") its [meaning](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/meaning#English "meaning"). coordinate terms ▲
	Coordinate terms: [subtext](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/subtext#English "subtext"), [paratext](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/paratext#English "paratext"), [peritext](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/peritext#English "peritext"), [epitext](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/epitext#English "epitext")
	*Without **context**, I can't tell if *[dish](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/dish#English "dish")* refers to a plate, or to the food served thereon.*
3. ([archaeology](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/archaeology#English "archaeology")) The [surroundings](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/surrounding#English "surrounding") and [environment](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/environment#English "environment") in which an [artifact](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/artifact#English "artifact") is found and which may provide important clues about the artifact's [function](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/function#English "function") and/or cultural [meaning](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/meaning#English "meaning").
4. ([mycology](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/mycology#English "mycology")) The [trama](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/trama#English "trama") or [flesh](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/flesh#English "flesh") of a [mushroom](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/mushroom#English "mushroom").
5. ([logic](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/logic#English "logic")) *For a formula*: a finite set of [variables](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/variable#English "variable"), which set contains all the [free variables](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/free_variable#English "free variable") in the given formula.
6. ([programming](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/programming#Noun "programming")) The [data](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/data#English "data") ([register](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/register#English "register") contents, [program counter](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/program_counter#English "program counter") value, etc.) needed to [switch](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/switch#English "switch") to another [thread](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/thread#English "thread") of [execution](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/execution#English "execution").
