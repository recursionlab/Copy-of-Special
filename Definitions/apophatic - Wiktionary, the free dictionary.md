### Adjective

\[[edit](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=apophatic&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Adjective")\]

**apophatic** (*[comparative](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#comparative "Appendix:Glossary")* **more **apophatic****, *[superlative](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#superlative "Appendix:Glossary")* **most **apophatic****)

1. ([theology](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/theology#English "theology")) Pertaining to knowledge of [God](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/God#English "God") obtained through [negation](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/negation#English "negation") rather than positive assertions. quotations ▼
2. () That [passively](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/passive#English "passive") defines a thing by describing what is not [characteristic](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/characteristic#English "characteristic") of it. quotations ▼
