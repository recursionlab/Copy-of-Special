### Noun

\[[edit](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=classifier&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Noun")\]

**classifier** (*plural* **[classifiers](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/classifiers#English "classifiers")**)

1. Someone who [classifies](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/classify#English "classify"). quotations ▼
2. ([linguistics](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/linguistics#English "linguistics")) A [word](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/word#English "word") or [morpheme](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/morpheme#English "morpheme") used in some [languages](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/language#English "language") (such as [CJKV](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/CJKV#English "CJKV") languages and [sign languages](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sign_language#English "sign language")), in certain [contexts](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/context#English "context") (such as [counting](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/counting#English "counting")), to indicate the [semantic](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/semantic#English "semantic") [class](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/class#English "class") to which something [belongs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/belong#English "belong").
3. A [machine](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/machine#English "machine") that separates particles or objects of different size or density.
4. ([computing](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/computing#Noun "computing")) A [program](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/program#English "program") or [algorithm](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/algorithm#English "algorithm") that classifies.
5. ([object-oriented programming](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/object-oriented_programming#English "object-oriented programming")) A [metaclass](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/metaclass#English "metaclass") used to group other entities having common [features](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/feature#English "feature"). quotations ▼
