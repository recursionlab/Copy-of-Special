## English

### Etymology

From [Middle English](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_English "w:Middle English") *[sours](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sours#Middle_English "sours")*, from [Old French](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_French "w:Old French") *[sorse](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sorse#Old_French "sorse")* (“rise, beginning, spring, source”), from *[sors](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sors#Old_French "sors")*, past participle of *[sordre](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=sordre&action=edit&redlink=1 "sordre (page does not exist)")*, *[sourdre](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sourdre#Old_French "sourdre")*, from [Latin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latin "w:Latin") *[surgō](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/surgo#Latin "surgo")* (“to rise”), which is composed of *[sub-](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sub-#Latin "sub-")* (“up from below”) + *[regō](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/rego#Latin "rego")* (“lead, rule”), ultimately from [Proto-Indo-European](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proto-Indo-European_language "w:Proto-Indo-European language") *[\*h₃réǵeti](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/h%E2%82%83r%C3%A9%C7%B5eti "Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/h₃réǵeti")* (“to straighten; right”), from the root *[\*h₃reǵ-](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/h%E2%82%83re%C7%B5- "Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/h₃reǵ-")*. [Doublet](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#doublet "Appendix:Glossary") of *[surge](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/surge#English "surge")*.

### Noun

**source** (*plural* **[sources](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sources#English "sources")**)

1. The [person](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/person#English "person"), [place](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/place#English "place"), or [thing](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/thing#English "thing") from which something (information, goods, etc.) [comes](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/come#English "come") or is [acquired](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/acquire#English "acquire"). synonym ▲ quotations ▼
	Synonym: [fund](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/fund#English "fund") (formal)
	*The accused refused to reveal the **source** of the illegal drugs she was selling.*
2. [Spring](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/spring#English "spring"); [fountainhead](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/fountainhead#English "fountainhead"); [wellhead](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/wellhead#English "wellhead"); any collection of water on or under the surface of the ground in which a stream originates. quotations ▼
	*The main **sources** of the Euphrates River are the Karasu and Murat Rivers.*
3. A reporter's [informant](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/informant#English "informant").
4. ([computing](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/computing#Noun "computing")) [Source code](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/source_code#English "source code").
5. ([electronics](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/electronics#English "electronics")) The name of one [terminal](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/terminal#English "terminal") of a [field effect](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/field_effect#English "field effect") [transistor](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/transistor#English "transistor") ([FET](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/FET#English "FET")).
6. ([graph theory](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/graph_theory#English "graph theory")) A [node](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/node#English "node") in a [directed graph](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/directed_graph#English "directed graph") whose edges all go out from it; one with no entering edges.
7. ([mathematics](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/mathematics#English "mathematics"), [category theory](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/category_theory#English "category theory")) The [domain](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/domain#English "domain") of a function; the [object](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/object#English "object") which a [morphism](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/morphism#English "morphism") points from. coordinate term ▲
	Coordinate term: [target](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/target#English "target")
