### Etymology 1

From [Middle English](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_English "w:Middle English") *[mening](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/mening#Middle_English "mening")*, *[menyng](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=menyng&action=edit&redlink=1 "menyng (page does not exist)")*, equivalent to + *[\-ing](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/-ing#English "-ing")*. Cognate with [Scots](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scots_language "w:Scots language") *[mening](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/mening#Scots "mening")* (“intent, purpose, sense, meaning”), [West Frisian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Frisian_language "w:West Frisian language") *[miening](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=miening&action=edit&redlink=1 "miening (page does not exist)")* (“opinion, mind”), [Dutch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dutch_language "w:Dutch language") *[mening](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/mening#Dutch "mening")* (“view, opinion, judgement”), [German](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/German_language "w:German language") *[Meinung](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Meinung#German "Meinung")* (“opinion, view, mind, idea”), [Danish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Danish_language "w:Danish language") and [Swedish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swedish_language "w:Swedish language") *[mening](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/mening#Swedish "mening")* (“meaning, sense, sentence, opinion”), [Icelandic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Icelandic_language "w:Icelandic language") *[meining](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/meining#Icelandic "meining")* (“meaning”).

#### Noun

**meaning** (*[countable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#countable "Appendix:Glossary") and [uncountable](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable "Appendix:Glossary")*, *plural* **[meanings](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/meanings#English "meanings")**)

1. (of [words](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/words#English "words"), [expressions](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/expressions#English "expressions") or [symbols](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/symbols#English "symbols"))
	1. The [denotation](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/denotation#English "denotation"), [referent](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/referent#English "referent"), or [idea](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/idea#English "idea") connected with a [word](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/word#English "word"), [expression](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/expression#English "expression"), or [symbol](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/symbol#English "symbol"). quotations ▼
		2. The [connotation](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/connotation#English "connotation") associated with a word, expression, or symbol.
2. The [purpose](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/purpose#English "purpose"), [value](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/value#English "value"), or [significance](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/significance#English "significance") (of something) beyond the fact of that thing's [existence](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/existence#English "existence").
	*the **meaning** of life*
	*The number of persons attending the vigil had a lot of **meaning** to the families.*
3. (of a person's actions) [Intention](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/intention#English "intention"). quotations ▼
