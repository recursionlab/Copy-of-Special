## English

### Etymology

[Borrowed](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#loanword "Appendix:Glossary") from [Latin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latin "w:Latin") *[inter-](#Latin)* (“between, amid”), a form of prepositional (“between”).
