In [mathematics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathematics "Mathematics"), **topology** is a branch of [geometry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geometry "Geometry") concerned with the study of [topological spaces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Topological_space "Topological space"). The term *topology* is also used for a set of open sets used to define [topological spaces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Topological_space "Topological space"). See the [topology glossary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Topology_glossary "Topology glossary") for common terms and their definition. Properties of general topological spaces (as opposed to [manifolds](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manifold "Manifold")) are discussed in [general topology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_topology "General topology").
